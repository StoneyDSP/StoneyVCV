## Panel

### inputs

#### in

Standard input.

Supports polyphony.

#### cv

Control Voltage input.

Supports polyphony.

### outputs

#### out

Standard output.

Supports polyphony.

### parameters

#### gain

Control voltage gain multiplier.

### lights

#### blink

Shows a smoothed representation of the sum total of the voltage at all `out` ports.
